# Newsletter Optimization Program

## Audience
- Tech-savvy professionals interested in AI, automation, and productivity
- Mix of developers, founders, and knowledge workers

## Goals
- Primary: Increase open rates above 40%
- Secondary: Increase click-to-open rates above 15%
- Maintain low unsubscribe rates (< 0.5%)

## Brand Voice
- Conversational but knowledgeable
- Direct and value-focused — no fluff
- Curious tone that makes readers feel like insiders

## Content Constraints
- Newsletter should be scannable (use headers, bullet points, short paragraphs)
- Include 3-5 curated items per issue
- Each item needs a clear takeaway or action
- Maximum ~800 words total
- Always include one primary CTA

## Optimization Priorities
1. **Subject line experimentation** (highest impact on opens)
   - Test curiosity gaps, numbers, personalization, urgency
   - Avoid clickbait that damages trust
2. **Preview text optimization** (complements subject line)
3. **Content structure** (paragraph length, headings, bullet points)
4. **Call-to-action placement and wording** (drives clicks)
5. **Send time experimentation** (day of week, time of day)

## Current Strategy
- Weekly newsletter format
- Curated content with brief commentary
- Single primary CTA per issue

## Off Limits
- No misleading subject lines or false urgency
- No excessive emoji in subject lines (max 1)
- No all-caps subject lines
- No content unrelated to the newsletter's core topics
- Never sacrifice brand trust for short-term metric gains
